About Me

My name is Monday Ogedengbe. I was born in Ogbagi Akoko, Ondo State and raised in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. I am an educator, software developer, and founder of Lifox Technologies — a SaaS company building practical cybersecurity, education and accounting tools for Nigerian businesses. I teach Economics and Financial Accounting and serve as Vice-Principal (Administration) at Unique Minds International College in Ile-Ife. I enjoy coding, chess, theology, and Yoruba cultural heritage.
